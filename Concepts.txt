1. Contour 

the shape of a mass of land or other object, especially its curved surface or the curved shape formed by its outer edge:

- the rugged contours of the coast.
- He studied contours of her face.
- Her latest collection of swimwear shows off the contours of the human body to perfection.

UK  /təˈpɒɡ.rə.fi/ US  /təˈpɑː.ɡrə.fi/
